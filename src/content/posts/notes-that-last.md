The best notes are not always polished essays. Sometimes they are compact explanations of why a decision
was made, what was tried, and what should be remembered before changing it again.

## Two Readers

That shape works well for a personal blog because it respects both readers:

1. Someone arriving from the outside.
2. The author returning months later.

## Close to the Work

Static Markdown keeps those notes close to the code, versioned with the rest of the site, and easy to
move if the project grows in a different direction.
