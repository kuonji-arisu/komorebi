Reading pages benefit from quiet structure. A comfortable line length, predictable spacing, and clear
heading hierarchy make the interface feel composed without asking for attention.

## Rhythm Before Decoration

> The page should make the next sentence easy to enter.

## Useful Constraints

For a small site, the useful details are simple:

- A single content column.
- Enough contrast without harsh edges.
- Links, quotes, and code blocks that share the same visual language.

When those pieces agree with each other, the site can stay minimal without feeling unfinished.
